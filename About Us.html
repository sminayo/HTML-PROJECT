The center is established under Section 41 of the Children Act 2022 (review of Children Act 2001), to regulate, coordinate, and oversee children's homes.

Vision

To make children smile by providing basic needs and empowering them through access to quality education.

Our Goals

  • To provide orphans and vulnerable children with a place to live, clothing, food, and medical attention.
  • To enable children to acquire education and training opportunities.
  • To provide children with spiritual support, therapy, and guidance.
  • To promote and protect children's legal rights.
  • To teach children life skills that will help them integrate into society.
  • To protect children from abuse, neglect, and abandonment.
  • To offer counselling services to help children overcome stigma, rejection, and trauma.
  • To raise children in godly ways.
